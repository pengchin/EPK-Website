7. There is beauty in human's heart

Please ask yourself now: If you found RMB 5,000 on the road, will you stand there for one hour waiting for someone to claim it? Or if you found a mobile phone on the road, will you return it to the owner?

When I lost my mobile phone in a taxi the other day, I almost did not have the hope of getting it back. As I believed that nowadays there are not many good guys as me *_* (this is my red face), people can be easily tempted by money, and mobile phones. You know how I was feeling standing there: that I lost a mobile phone by no reason, it costed 2 thousand bucks, my friends will not find me through the phone for a while...

But when I phoned my mobile phone, the driver actually answered the phone and said he is there waiting for me to get the phone back!

I salute this driver for his kind heart and I do hope that I will not hesitate in returning the RMB 5,000 to the right owner.
