11. Piece of PR For Your Thoughts

BEC and Legend are promoting the Ericsson's WebSwitch. How to get the most media publicity? If Intel now is selling Pentium V, the news papers will have a whole page article describing the story. Can our media briefing have the same effect?

Not every company has the product that is of great interest to the news papers but still we have to try to see if there can be a larger-than-very-small-size article or an article different than the custom made news release appearing in the news paper.

As for the BEC's WebSwitch, we invited 3 journalists to dinner before the media briefing, we briefed them, and then they took part in the media briefing. Afterwards, 2 of the journalists said they will try to make something bigger for us.

At least this is a possibility, right?


12. A Road Killer?

The other day I met a friend and asked her how many staff her company has, she started by: herself, a driver.... A driver? ! I asked her why her small company has to employ a driver.

As it is now, more and more people can drive, and so more and more companies are not employing drivers to only do the driver's job.

That's why I enrolled myself in a driving school. Some people is only trying to get a driving license, but I think I'd better stay there and use up all the teaching hours that they would offer me, if only for my own safety reason.

A friend quipped that there would be another road killer on the road, but considering that I'm spending this much time in the training, the possibility is not that big, right?

By the way, nowadays, there aren't many people as polite as me: yesterday, my new driving teacher told me in my face that my driving skills are really bad considering that I've spent 16 hours in the school!
