2. Computer and Efficiency and, and, and Lucia

Computer is a great tool for one's work. They speed things up. But if they do not keep fit then they give their owners trouble.

I have been having a lot of trouble recently with my computer or computers, as I had already used 3 computers since I came to Aboda. Certainly before I joined in, the computers I used had not greatly served my work-their capabilities had not been very much utilized. That's perhaps the problems now with the computers I'm using. We expect them to do a lot of things, they got too occupied and then they got broken down somewhere along the way.

The one who is also likely to get a break-down is Lucia, who must very much hate the computers by now, whenever we have problems with the computers she has to be there. I'm somewhat ashamed, or at least not proud, that when my computer broke down, I had to call her for help. Poor Peng-ignorant, poor Lucia-Bad luck.

But she will learn a lot of things from the computer repairing...



3. What is the most important thing in life?

Though it helps if you are a millionaire, but the most important thing is not that you are a millionaire, or you have a house in Beijing or you are a permanent Beijing resident. The most important thing is that you are an excellent person and you have a kind heart: eager to help others, have a decent mind and heart, that's the beauty in life.


4. Oh, my god, 18th floor!

It was already 4 o'clock in the morning of 29th when we finally finished our preparation for that day's BEC Ip Gateway launch. I was really worried about how I'm going to go back to my room, as my room was on the top of the world-the 18th floor.

But my room I should go, that's where I can only have a sweet and sound sleep.

I started climbing the stairs, one floor, another floor, 10th floor, though I was panting, but I actually climbed all the way up to the top, and amazingly I was not very tired, only breathing hard. If you think, that our office is on the 5th floor, and if you climb 2 times at a time, that's 10 floors, and 18 floors? That's only 8 floors to go, sounds not too many?

And so, 18 floors is not a big deal to me and I should be able to allow myself another climbing should I arrive home late again.

PS: strong protest for the rules which stopped the elevator after 12 o'clock, this violates my human rights!
